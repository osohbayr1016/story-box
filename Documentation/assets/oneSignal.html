OneSignal Setup

Requirements

  • To create a OneSignal account, follow these steps:
  1. Go to theOneSignal Website.
  2. Click on the "Get Started" button or "Sign Up" link.
  3. Fill out the registration form with your email, password, and any other required information.
  4. Verify your email address by clicking on the verification link sent to your email.
  5. Once verified, log in to your OneSignal account.

IOS Setup

  • Please ensure you have the following requirements to set up your iOS project for OneSignal:
  1. An iOS 11+ or iPadOS 11+ device (iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch) for testing purposes. Alternatively, an Xcode 14+ simulator running iOS 16+ can be used.
  2. A Mac with Xcode 12+ installed.
  • Follow theprovided guideto set up your iOS project accordingly.

Android Setup

  • To configure your Android project for OneSignal, you'll need the following:
  1. An Android device or emulator running Android 5.0 or higher, with "Google Play Store (Services)" installed.
  • Please follow theprovided guideto set up your Android project accordingly.
  • To obtain the Android notification channel ID, please refer tothe provided guide.
  • To change the small icon for Android notifications, please follow the instructionsprovided in the guide.

How To Get OneSignal App ID And Key

  • Please refer to theprovided guideto locate and copy your OneSignal App ID and Key.

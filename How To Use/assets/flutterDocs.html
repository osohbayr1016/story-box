Introduction

  • Hey there, This is for StoryBox app documentation to know the app in better way and how to use it.

Login Page

  • Continue as a Guest: Explore the app without signing in.
  • Sign in with Apple: Use your Apple ID for quick access.
  • Sign in with Google: Log in via your Google account.

Home Tab

  • Featured Slider: Highlights popular content with a "Play" button.
  • Most Trending: Displays top shows/movies with rankings.
  • Continue Watching: Easily resume previously watched content.
  • New Releases & Categories: Organized sections like "New Releases," "Some Suspense," and "Love at First Sight."
  • Bottom Navigation: Access Home, Library, Gifts, and Profile.

Reels Screen

  • Video Playback: Displays engaging reels with a play button for interaction.
  • Engagement Options: Includes options to like, add to the list, and share reels.
  • Content Details: Shows the reel's title and a brief description below the video.

Episode-Wise Reels Screen

  • Video Playback: Features episode-specific reels with playback controls and progress tracking.
  • Engagement Options: Includes like, add-to-list, and share buttons for interaction.
  • Episode List: Displays all episodes in a numbered grid, with locked episodes requiring access through ads, coins, or VIP subscription.
  • Unlock Options: Users can:
    • Watch ads for free access.
    • Use coins from their balance.
    • Purchase a VIP plan for unlimited access.
    • Auto-Play Feature: Users can enable auto-unlock for the next episode.

MyListview Tab

  • My List: Displays user-added shows/movies for quick access. Shows episode progress (e.g., EP.0/EP.8).
  • History: Lists previously watched content in chronological order. Includes titles and episodes for reference.

Earn reward

  • Daily Check-In:
    • Users can check in daily to collect coins.
    • Missed days are marked as "Lost."
    • Successful check-ins display a pop-up with the coins earned (e.g., "+8 Coins").
  • Watch Ads: Users can earn additional coins by watching ads, with each ad offering a specific coin amount (e.g., +3, +5, +10).

Profile tab

User Information:
  • Displays user details like name, UID, and wallet balance.
Wallet Management:
  • Shows coin balance with a "Refill" option.
  • Access to transaction history, unlocked episodes, and reward coin history.
Settings Menu:
  • Language: Choose from various languages like English, Hindi, and more.
Account Options:
  • View Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  • Delete account with a detailed confirmation process.
  • Sign out with a logout confirmation prompt.
Subscription Management:
  • VIP plans and refill options for coins are available with pricing details.

Search screen

  • Search Bar: Users can type titles, themes, or keywords to find content.
  • Popular Searches: Displays trending and commonly searched content under "People are searching."
  • Search Results: Shows relevant results with titles, themes, and brief descriptions.
